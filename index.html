How to Pay

We accept M-Pesa. Quick, safe, and confirmed within minutes.

01
Choose your watch
Browse the collection and click “Enquire” to chat with us on WhatsApp. We’ll confirm availability and reserve your piece.
02
Get payment details
After confirming on WhatsApp, we’ll send you the exact amount and our M-Pesa Till or Paybill number.
03
Pay via M-Pesa
M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill → Business number → Amount → Confirm with PIN.
04
Send confirmation
Screenshot your M-Pesa message and send it to us on WhatsApp. Your watch will be dispatched same day.
